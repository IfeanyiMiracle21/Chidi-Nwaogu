About Me

I am Chidi Nwaogu, an award-winning entrepreneur, ecosystem builder, and software engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria. With a passion for leveraging technology to address global challenges, I have founded and scaled ventures like Savvy, Publiseer, and LAGbook, earning recognition as one of Nigeria's 100 Most Powerful Young Leaders and a key figure in African entrepreneurship. My journey includes authoring the comprehensive guide "Dear Entrepreneur" and mentoring thousands of young innovators through programs like the Tony Elumelu Foundation and YALI. I am driven by a commitment to empower the next generation and foster sustainable impact across emerging markets.
